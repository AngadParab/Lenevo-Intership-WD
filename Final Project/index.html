Protection Strategies

Practical steps to safeguard yourself and your business

For Individuals

  • Be alert to UPI / online payment frauds ‚Äì always verify the sender before transferring money.
  • Report suspicious calls or SMS to the 1930 Cyber Crime Helpline
  • Never share OTP, CVV, or banking PINs, even with supposed ‚Äúbank staff‚Äù.
  • Check for fake job offers and lottery scams common on WhatsApp & Facebook.
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi banking transactions (cafes, beaches, etc.).
  • install sanchar saathi app / Update helpline contacts on your phone

For Businesses

  • Conduct regular cyber safety awareness workshops for employees.
  • Secure POS machines and digital payment apps from skimming/malware.
  • Use strong access controls for accounting & customer data.
  • Back up data securely to prevent ransomware lockouts.
  • Partner with Goa Police Cyber Cell for prevention training and quick response.
  • Encourage customers to use secure digital wallets & trusted apps only
