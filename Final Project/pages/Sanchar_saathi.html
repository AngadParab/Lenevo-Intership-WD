About Sanchar Saathi

Sanchar Saathi is an initiative by the Department of Telecommunications (DoT), Government of India, designed to empower citizens in securing their mobile connections. It helps users track lost or stolen mobile phones and block them to prevent misuse.

Key Features:

  • Track and block lost/stolen mobile devices.
  • Verify mobile connections issued in your name.
  • Report fraud or suspicious SIM activity.
  • Ensure safer mobile communication in India.
