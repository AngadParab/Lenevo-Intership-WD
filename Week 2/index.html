Goa State Action Plan on Climate Change

The Goa State Action Plan for Climate Change sets out clear strategies to address the effects of climate change in the state. In line with national guidelines, the plan identifies key priority actions, responsible stakeholders, and essential resources to help Goa adapt to and mitigate climate impacts across sectors. Resilient Cities & Coastal Areas

Goa faces sea level rise and increased flooding due to climate change. Local actions are focused on strengthening embankments, restoring mangroves, and ensuring climate-smart city planning to safeguard coastal communities.

Everyday Green Choices

From replacing streetlights with LEDs to promoting electric vehicles and enforcing plastic bans, Goans are making sustainable choices daily. Community composting and solar initiatives are growing across the state.
